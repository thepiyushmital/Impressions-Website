Kavya

A poet is a person who is passionately in love with the language. So, if you are brimming with buried feelings or thoughts that are needed to be heard, weave them into beautiful words and read them aloud.

Rules

1. This module consists of two rounds
2.Round 1: Participant will have to pick a topic on draw basis at the venue and will be given 30 minutes time to compose a poem
3.Round 2: Participant will have to recite his/her earlier own composed poem along with poem composed in Round 1
4. Language used can be Hindi or Marathi only
5. Participants have to report 1 hour before the competition
6. Registration fees: Rs 100 per person
7. Team size : Solo event
8. Time duration : 4-6min
