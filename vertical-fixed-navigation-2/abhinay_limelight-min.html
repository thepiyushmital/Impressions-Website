Limelight

It's time for the many different actors in you to come out and showcase themselves to stir up various emotions in the audience. Make them smile, make them cry and make them remember you forever.

Rules

1. This module consists of one round
2. Participants are free to perform a script or passage of their choice
3. Act should be in Hindi, Marathi or English
4. Participants would be provided with a chair and table
5. Individual properties are allowed based on the discretion of the organisers
6. The performance will be judged purely on acting.No lights and music will be provided
7. Registration fees: Rs 100 per person
8. Team size: Solo event
9. Time duration : 5 min (+1 min preparation time)
