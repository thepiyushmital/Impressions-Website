About Us

IMPRESSIONS - the annual cultural fest of College of Engineering, Pune, is taking baby steps and stepping into it's second edition this year. After a great success of the first edition that attracted a whooping number of almost 10,000 people from across the country, WE are back! Promising even more this year. Boxed with several intriguing and stupefying events, the hard working Cultural Club of CoEP, has brought to you five exciting modules - Arts and Crafts, Dance, Music, Drama and Camera. Impressions aims to provide everyone with the most memorable time of their lives, simultaneously providing them with a platform so varied that they have a thousand ways to express their art. We hope to make this landmark initiative the melting pot of Cultural activities for colleges in times to come. We like to welcome you as a part of Impressions '17 taking place on 9th, 10th and 11th December 2017 at College of Engineering, Pune. Come and celebrate the glory of art with us!
